About the D4 Brothers

The "D4 Brothers" are Doel Salvador R. Mercado, Doel Diego R. Mercado, Domiel May R. Mercado, and Doel Donato R. Mercado, listed here in birth order. They are the children of Domingo L. Mercado and Elvira G. Recepcion and are known for their diverse talents as athletes, artists, and academics.

Raised primarily by their mother, a chemical engineer and former university professor at the University of Saint Anthony, she now works as a chef specializing in Italian and other European cuisines.

Doel Salvador R. Mercado, the eldest, was a Philippine Science High School scholar and now works as a researcher in forensic science. Doel Diego R. Mercado is a multi-awarded champion in pub games, including billiards and darts, and is known for his steady hands and exceptional basketball skills, shooting around 90% in free throws. In his teens, he also played Sepak Takraw and currently applies his quick reflexes to mobile gaming.

Domiel May, the unica hija and only sister, has focused on her artistic talents. She has experience as an actress, dancer, and model and is now a visual artist, creating art alongside her children, Carmina Victoria and Marco Messi. Both Doel Diego and Domiel May have modeled in pageants.

Doel Donato, the youngest, excelled in academics and sports from an early age. He was an honor student throughout grade school and even tutored older students while still in Grade 1. Athletically gifted, he represented his school in multiple sports and competed in badminton at the Palarong Pambansa. Currently, he works as a head chef in various restaurants in the Philippines and abroad, specializing in Oriental cuisine.

All four siblings are visual artists who have competed in national art competitions.
